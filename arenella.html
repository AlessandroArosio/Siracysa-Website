Seaside resort Arenella

One of the most popular beach of the coast of Siracusa

The Arenella beach fits in any touristic needs: it's very big and it's made of light and fine granulation sand. The bathing establishment offers the possibility of renting beach umbrellas and sunbeds, canoes and pedalo. There is also a new-built big resort. The beach is well-equipped, with a bar and restaurant inside of it. On the left side of the establishment there is a small public beach and a solarium built on the sea. On the right side of the establishment there is a beautiful public beach and few meters away there is a rock beach.

  • Kind of the shore coast: SAND
  • Distance from Siracusa: 10 Km
  • Public or private beach: public and private
  • Parking area: free parking and private parking
  • Accessible facilities: available
